About Species

HOW A CANDELIQUE IS "BORN"

How they are made and how they reproduce

Candeliques are created by higher beings that call themselves "Chandlers", a term used for candle makers. They start with the crystal heart and create from the inside out. Candelique lovers are able to seek out a Chandler for aid in creating a child. The Chandler wil use the flames from the lover's tail candles and use it to light the flame of the crystal heart of the infant Candelique. Same sex couples are capable of having a child since all they need is their flame.

The lifespan of a Candelique

And how they can die

While Candeliques are not immortal, they have very long life spans. The oldest recorded Candelique was 800 years old, which is 80 years old in human years. Their regeneration is faster than a human's, a gash in their arm can heal in just a couple hours. This is due to their blood being made of hot wax that cauderizes any bleeding and speeds up the making of new skin. However their weakspot is the window on their chest that leads to their fragile crystal heart. Breaking it will immediately kill the Candelique and it's flame horns and tail will go out. Trying to break it with your hands however will give you severe burns and cuts.

A Candelique's Purpose

their main task

Due to an overpopulation of lost souls in purgatory, Candeliques are tasked with finding souls and passing judgement. If the soul is pure, they are escorted to paradise. However if the soul is evil, they are extinguished by the Candelique's snuffing staff and removed from existence. This is how Candeliques earn Wax stamps, which is their currency (treated like coins). Some candeliques have a preference on which souls they seek out, some going for pure souls over evil souls and vice versa.
